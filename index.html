Setup instructions

    If Tivimate is your prefered IPTV player, you will need to follow the following steps:

  • Add your Datoo/LiveGo service to Tivimate via Xtream Codes option in the "Add Playlist" option. This will add the service with everything in it as is.
  • Provide your USERNAME & PASSWORD in the form here to download your updated copy of M3U file.
  • Transfer the M3U file from your computer to your Android TV device (plenty of guides on how to do it are available online).
  • Within TiviMate, go to: Settings > EPG > EPG sources > Add source > Enter URL
  • Type the following URL: https://bit.ly/it999ru and complete the EPG addition.
    1. This will add the it999.ru EPG source so we can add it to the playlist. HOT channels are not supported by the updated playlist I am using so when this service goes back online, if mapped to the playlist, it will automatically load the EPG for those channels.
  • Go to: Settings > Playlists > Add playlist > M3U playlist > Select local playlist > Internal shared storage > [navigate to the M3U file location on your device and select it] > complete the addition of the playlist by simply approving the defaults.
  • After being added, you will not see the DikoPlus listed on your playlists.
  • Go into the DikoPlus playlist > EPG sources > [make sure the following EPG sources are now enabled for this playlist]:
    • github.com - should be enabled by default.
      1. This is added automatically by the M3U playlist and most of the channels pull their EPG from it.
    • bit.ly - enable for this playlist (this is the one we added earlier).
      1. As stated previously, this is the it999.ry EPG source. only here for HOT and a couple more channels.
  • Please note that after the first time of adding the playlist and adding the EPG sources to it, it will take a few minutes to complete updating all the TV guide listings.
